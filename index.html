Comp-Sci had me at
"Hello World!" <3

I started programming when I was 18. Sounds pretty late in this day and age, right? Unfortunately, my high school didn't offer a single computer science course. The department of Computing didn't exist! As a result, I was the only girl in my entire graduating class of about 350 students that pursued a degree in computer science.

So how did I end up in computer science? My answer: fate. One special engineer appeared in my life and she was all it took to sway me into this beautiful field. I've fallen in love and have been practicing ever since, starting with this website!
